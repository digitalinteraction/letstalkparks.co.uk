Events

These upcoming Let’s Talk Parks events will provide you with opportunities to explore specific aspects of the council’s proposals for Newcastle’s parks, and share your ideas via three strands of activities: workshops, Twitter discussions, and the ‘explore’ section of the Let’s Talk Parks website.

These activities will run from 13 March-22 April. Find out more and sign up for one of our upcoming workshops.

Sign up here for workshops

 

Views and ideas collected from all of these activities will be used to shape Newcastle City Council proposals for Newcastle’s Parks. You can also contribute your views anytime through Newcastle City Council's online survey on Let’s talk Newcastle here.
