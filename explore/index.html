How it works

Local government budget reductions mean that it is increasingly challenging to care for the parks and allotments. We need to find a new way to look after them sustainably for future generations.

See the questions that Newcastle City Council is facing and some possible ways to answer them. Remember these are works-in-progress and can change!

On each page, you will find ideas from the workshops, live twitter discussions and other people. You can vote on any of these ideas and opinions!

Share your ideas!

Our parks are in poor state so in the spirit of generosity, if you do not like or agree with something, share your ideas on how this could work better or differently!

 

The aim is to find, together, new and viable solutions to look after the parks and allotments we all love!  Before you start, if you haven’t done it yet, have a look at the Council’s proposal here!
