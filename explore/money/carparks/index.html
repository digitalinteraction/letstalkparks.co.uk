Car Parks

Car parks could be a way of securing a reliable source of income for a future parks charity. Car parking could be expanded on selected sites and charges implemented. 

More Info

Some of our parks cannot easily be accessed by foot or by public transport.  So, some of our parks have car parking to ensure that those who need or wish to visit by road can do so. Yet, there is also evidence that cars have a detrimental impact on our environment.

Is increasing car parks and charges a good way forward?
